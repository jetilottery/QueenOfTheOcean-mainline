Mistress of the Sea

Are you ready to play a Match 3 game? Mistress of the Sea gives you 6 rounds to collect the gems for each ticket.

Try to get 3 identical gems in a round to win the associated prize. If you get a star gem, your win the associated prize immediately.

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to automatically play the game to completion. Game play continues until all selections have been made.

How to Play

The object of Mistress of the Sea is to find 3 identical gems in a round. Find a star gem in a round, win the corresponding prize instantly.

At the start of the game, Mistress of the Sea displays 6 rounds, each round with 5 maps. Selecting a map to reveal a gem, and using REVEAL ALL has the game play to completion automatically.

If the player finds:

  • 3 identical gems in a round, he or she wins the prize of that round.
  • A star gem, he or she wins the prize of that round.

To complete play, the player must select all maps or use REVEAL ALL, after which a message appears indicating the game’s outcome, and play ends.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins are shown in currency.
